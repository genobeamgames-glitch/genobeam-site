Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 25, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Geno Beam Studio ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile games and related services (collectively, the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve the Service. This includes:

  • Device Information: Your device model, operating system, unique device identifiers (including Advertising ID), and mobile network information.
  • Usage Information: Details about how you use the Service, such as your game progress, levels, scores, achievements, and interactions with game features.
  • Log and Location Information: We collect server logs, which include your IP address to determine your general location (e.g., country).
  • Purchase Information: If you make in-app purchases, we collect information about those transactions. We do not collect or store your credit card information.
  • Information from Third-Party Accounts: If you log in using a service like Google Play Games or Apple Game Center, we may access basic profile information as authorized by that service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Service.
  • To save your game progress and data.
  • To respond to your customer service requests.
  • To analyze trends and usage to improve the player experience.
  • To show advertisements within the Service (contextual or personalized).

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Partners providing cloud hosting (e.g., AWS) and analytics.
  • Advertising Partners: To provide advertisements. Partners may include Google AdMob, Unity Ads, AppLovin, Meta Audience Network, and IronSource.
  • Legal Reasons: If required by law or to protect rights and safety.

4. Children's Privacy

In compliance with COPPA, we implement an age gate. For users under 13, we do not collect personal information for personalized ads and only show contextual advertising.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Accessing/Deleting Data: You can request a copy or deletion of your data.
  • Opting Out: Use your device settings ("Limit Ad Tracking" on iOS or "Opt out of Ads Personalization" on Android).

6. Data Security and Retention

We take reasonable measures to protect your information and retain it as long as your account is active.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time by posting the new version in the Service.
